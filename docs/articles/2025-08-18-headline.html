In addition to these issues, global security concerns, including conflicts in regions such as Afghanistan and the Middle East, have been a focus of discussions at the UN General Assembly. Leaders have been highlighting the importance of diplomacy and dialogue in resolving conflicts and promoting peace and stability around the world.
